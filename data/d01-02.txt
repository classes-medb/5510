#### D01-02. Identify dichotomies in a peer-reviewed paper

Graded: 3 points, 1 point deduction if late.

Find a peer-reviewed research paper that is relevant to your work or area of study. This must be a paper with full free text available. Post the abstract of this article and a link to the full text. Review the dichotomies in the video and classify the paper into one of the two levels in each dichotomy, or classify it as a mixture of both levels of the dichotomies. If you are unsure about any classification, be honest about your uncertainty.
